Contact

Pick the channel that matches your topic — it's the fastest way to get the right answer.

Channels

  • App support — support@khassinx.com. Bugs, questions, feedback. See Support for what to include.
  • Security vulnerabilities — security@khassinx.com. Coordinated disclosure per our security policy and security.txt.
  • Legal — legal@khassinx.com. Privacy rights, copyright notices, and correspondence from authorities.
  • Everything else — hello@khassinx.com. General inquiries, partnerships, press.

Languages

We correspond in English and Español only. Messages in other languages may go unanswered or receive a reply in English.

Response times

Support: 2–3 business days. Security reports: acknowledged within 5 business days. Legal requests: acknowledged within 5 business days.

Who you are writing to

Vellichor is published by KHASSINX LLC, a Florida limited liability company (United States). Legal correspondence is received by email at legal@khassinx.com.

A note on privacy: emailing us shares your email address with us. We use it only to reply — no lists, no marketing, no sharing. Details in the Privacy Policy.

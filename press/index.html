One-line description

Vellichor reads, summarizes, and answers questions from your PDFs using Apple Intelligence — entirely on your device.

One-paragraph description

Vellichor is a PDF Q&A app for Apple platforms that uses Apple Intelligence Foundation Models running on your device to summarize documents, search them semantically, and answer your questions with citations to the source page. Every answer is grounded in the document. Nothing is uploaded to us — no PDFs sent to our servers, no queries logged, no analytics; sync, if enabled, uses only your own encrypted iCloud. Vellichor is universal across iPhone, iPad, Mac, and Apple Watch, with Apple Pencil annotation that transcribes your handwriting on-device. Pricing is a single one-time unlock, no subscription. For researchers, lawyers, engineers, clinicians, and anyone whose documents shouldn't be uploaded to a chatbot company.

What makes it different

  • Genuinely on-device AI — uses Apple Intelligence Foundation Models, not a cloud API with a privacy promise
  • Citation-required answers — every response links to the source page; no hallucinated facts
  • Universal Apple-native — designed for each device, not a stretched iPad app
  • Apple Pencil transcription — handwriting is transcribed on-device, never sent to a server or cloud AI
  • One-time price — no subscription, no data harvesting business model

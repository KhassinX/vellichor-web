Support

Every message gets a reply from a person. No bots, no ticket black holes.

How to reach us

Email support@khassinx.com. We aim to reply within 2–3 business days.

We provide support in English and Español only. Messages in other languages may go unanswered or receive a reply in English.

What to include

  • Your device and OS version (for example, "iPhone 17, iOS 26.5").
  • What you did, what you expected, and what happened instead.
  • A screenshot if it helps — crop out anything personal first.

Purchases and refunds

Vellichor's core features are free. Pro is a one-time unlock — no subscription. The price is shown in the app before you buy, the purchase is processed by Apple, and refunds are handled by Apple at reportaproblem.apple.com.

Security issues

Vulnerabilities don't go to support — use the dedicated channel: security@khassinx.com. Details at Security & Responsible Disclosure.

Ask your PDFs anything.
Privately.

Vellichor reads, summarizes, and answers questions from your PDFs using Apple Intelligence — entirely on your device. Papers, contracts, books, manuals. Your documents never leave your iPhone.

Coming soon to the App Store

Vellichor is built by KhassinX, an independent studio for privacy-first Apple platform apps.

How Vellichor is different

100% on-device

Apple Intelligence Foundation Models read your PDFs locally. The contents never reach a server, ours or anyone else's. No internet connection required.

Every answer cited

Every response links to the exact page in the source document. Tap a citation and you are there. No hallucinated facts hidden behind confident prose.

Apple-native everywhere

iPhone, iPad, Mac, and Apple Watch. Universal across your devices via your own iCloud. Built on iOS 26 Liquid Glass — not a stretched iPad app pretending to be Mac.

Apple Pencil annotation

Handwritten margin notes transcribed on-device. Your annotations become searchable text alongside the PDF, without sending a single stroke to the cloud.

Honest pricing

One-time unlock, fairly priced. No subscription. Family Sharing on by default — buy once, use across your family's devices.

Designed for confidential work

For people whose documents shouldn't be uploaded to a chatbot company: researchers, lawyers, engineers, clinicians. Privacy is the product, not a setting.

Ask your PDFs.
Privately.

Vellichor reads, summarizes, and answers questions about your PDFs using Apple Intelligence — entirely on your device. Papers, contracts, books, manuals. Your content is never sent to our servers or any cloud AI — we have none. Your files sync, if you choose, only through your own encrypted iCloud.

Coming soon to the App Store · $14.99 once · iPhone, iPad & Mac · Family Sharing

Vellichor is built by KhassinX, an independent studio for privacy-first Apple platform apps.

What makes Vellichor different

100% on device

Apple Intelligence Foundation Models read your PDFs locally. Your content reaches no server — not ours, not anyone's. Works offline.

Every answer cited

Each answer links to the exact page in the source document. Tap the citation and jump straight there. No invented facts dressed up as confident prose.

Native on every Apple device

iPhone, iPad, Mac — and an Apple Watch companion that hands your reading back to your iPhone. Widgets, Control Center, and Siri Shortcuts included. Built on iOS 26 Liquid Glass — not a stretched iPad app pretending to be a Mac.

Read and annotate

Open any PDF with native performance — search, outline, jump to any page. Four page moods for long reading: Parchment, Sepia, Gray, Night. Highlight passages and add notes; your original PDF stays untouched, and your marks travel with your own iCloud.

Honest pricing

One fair purchase, $14.99. No subscription. Family Sharing on by default — buy once, use it across your family's devices.

Built for confidential work

For people whose documents shouldn't be uploaded to a chatbot company: researchers, lawyers, engineers, clinicians. Privacy is the product, not a toggle.

What we don't do

No servers

Vellichor has no backend. There is no Vellichor server to store, see, or breach your documents. The privacy story isn't a setting — it's the architecture.

No tracking

Zero analytics by default. Zero advertising. Zero cookies. Zero behavioral tracking. App Privacy Label reads "Data Not Collected" because nothing is.

No subscription

$14.99 one-time unlock. Family Sharing on by default — one purchase covers up to six family members. No renewal, no surprise charge, no expiry.

No cloud AI

Apple Intelligence runs on your chip. Your queries, document content, and AI processing never go to our servers or any cloud AI — not to us, not to OpenAI, not to anyone.

Universal Apple

One purchase covers iPhone, iPad, and Mac — including the Apple Watch companion — with Family Sharing enabled. Available in English and Spanish (Spain and Latin America) at launch.

Requirements: iPhone 15 Pro or newer for Apple Intelligence features · iPad with Apple Intelligence support · Apple Silicon Mac (M1+) for AI features · iOS 26.5+, iPadOS 26.5+, macOS 26.5+.
